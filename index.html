Han Nay Aung's Home Page

Han Nay Aung received the B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in Information and Computer Sciences from Kwansei Gakuin University, Hyogo, Japan, in 2022 and 2024, respectively. He is currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree at the same university. He is a member of IEEE and IEICE.

My research focuses on advancing the development of high-speed and highly reliable networks that are directly related to the safety of users. Currently, I address three key areas: (1) designing network architectures using machine learning, (2) designing functional safety systems for in-vehicle networks, and (3) developing exploration and search algorithms for large-scale graphs.
